About Us

COMET Services is a career development, counseling, mentoring, and training setup that has been providing these mentioned services to the children of Indian soldiers and other needy students from remote places who are either deprived of such facilities or are not able to afford such services due to very expensive costs.

COMET Offerings.

01

COMET at Home

In this regular service mode, registered students from class 6 and above are taken through the full COMET cycle in a step-wise manner, which enormously adds to their career progression and transforms students into successful professionals. Tracking and reporting progress status with students, their supervisors, and parents. The ultimate goal for IT field begins as a student at school.

02

COMET at Study centre

This unique service is designed for students who are pursuing BCA, B.Sc., and B.Com degree courses and are staying in PGs or in rental houses. With this offering, we provide dorm facilities to such students who can stay there for their studies. For those students who want to make a career in the IT field, our advanced IT training will help to prepare best for an attractive and a lucrative career.

03

COMET at Schools

As part of this offering, we work in close collaboration with registered schools and do the counseling of all students and provide personalized COMET services to all students based on their skills and interest. Along with the success of every student, this service also owns glorifies the "wall of fame" of schools with success stories of the students which will directly help schools.
