Bestowing on the vast experience of more than two decades, COMET Services is a Hyderabad-based ed-tech career counseling organization. COMET services have been operational as a part-time social service for the last 25 years in different locations like Shillong, Delhi, Uttarakhand and Hyderabad, helping children of hundreds of Indian army personals and also to other needy people from remote places of Uttarakhand.

History

The story of COMET Services began as a voluntary service in 1995 (at Assam Rifles campus, Shillong), with mentoring and coaching of 2-3 students. With my ongoing persistent contributions and with some support from few professionals from different fields, it has now taken the shape of an ed-tech career counseling and training institute, helping 100s of needy students from various parts of the country like northeast Indian states, Delhi, Hyderabad, and Uttarakhand.


Today, we are privileged to connect with 1000s of students, which allows us to transform the career of the needy students into a glorious success story.

Why COMET Services

In most educational institutes, students are fully occupied with academic studies without considering career development. This flaw in our education system prohibits students in achieving their goals as per their best abilities. Such gaps related to proper educational guidance and career development needs are more severe in remote places, either due to environmental factors or due to financial reasons.


COMET service works towards resolving these gaps for the success of every student, by helping students in shaping their career plan best, and also helping educational institutes to glorify their wall of fame with success stories of ex-students.

COMET Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality, effective, and affordable counseling to help students in transforming their skills and abilities into success stories. We take the privilege to walk alongside our students as they navigate the uncertainties and challenges of life. Amid their challenges, we provide COMET support and work towards motivating and empowering all students to move ahead in their career path.


We also aim to enable educational institutes to have their glorifying wall of fame with success stories of students. We facilitate this by working with educational institutes collaboratively.

Comet Vision

Our sincere interest is to do best for every student, helping them to reach their full potential and realize their dreams, by choosing and following the right career path and achieving goals with excellence. We believe that good education is a basic need for every student.


This directly helps educational institutes also in glorifying their wall of fame and creating more success stories for the glory of the institute.

Social Responsibility

We strongly believe in performing social responsibilities which drive us to perform our duty in the best interests of our environment and society as a whole.. Hence, with our unique to guide students 16X7, we not only work towards transforming students' career into a success story, but we also pay additional care and attention to develop students into a good human being and a true citizen who care for the environment, society and nation as a whole.
